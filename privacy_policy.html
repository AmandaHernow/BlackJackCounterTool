Privacy Policy — Blackjack Counter Tool

Last updated: March 2026

Blackjack Counter Tool is a free, offline card counting aid developed by Amanda Hansson Hernow. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data collection

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information of any kind. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising networks, and no third-party SDKs are used.

Internet access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests during normal use. The only exception is if you choose to tap the PayPal donate link or the support email link in the Support section, which will open your device's browser or email app respectively. No data is sent by the app itself.

Local storage

The app saves one item to your device's local storage: your dark/light theme preference. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

The app does not request any device permissions.

Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact: bjcountertoolandroidsupport@hernow.net
